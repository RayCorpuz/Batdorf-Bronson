Batdorf & Bronson Coffee House

Our flagship coffeehouse is located in the heart of downtown Olympia and has been serving the finest coffees, espresso drinks, teas and locally baked pastries since 1986. The coffeehouse features ample seating, inviting couches and a cozy fireplace.

The Batdorf & Bronson Tasting Room is located in our roastery and offers a variety of coffees for our patrons to sample. Visitors may enjoy coffee from the major growing areas of Africa, Indonesia and Latin America.

4219 6th Avenue SE.

Lacey, WA 98503

(360) 359 - 4622

Mon - Fri: 7am to 5pm

The Batdorf and Bronson Coffee Bar is conveniently located across the street from the Lacey Transit Center. Stop by and enjoy a fresh cup of exceptional coffee or lunch. This location offers wonderful paninis and salads and a delicious selection of baked goodies from Sacchariferous Bakery and Left Bank Pastries.
